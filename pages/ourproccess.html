OUR PROCESS

THE THV PROCESS

STEP 1: GO WINDOW SHOPPING AT OUR GALLERIES

View our galleries too see which model you want! As you can see, there are many many options which will make yours very uniquely yours! We also offer completely from-scratch custom tiny homes, some of which can be viewed in our Custom Gallery.

STEP 2: SEE YOUR BASE COST ON THE QUOTE PAGE

After selecting your model floor plan, work through our QuoteS to determine exactly how much you’ll need to build the tiny house of your dreams! If you would prefer a custom model, we will need to invest a decent amount of time into providing you an estimate.

The process is as follows:

  1. Pay $250 via PayPal or an alternative

  2. Email contact@moderntinyliving.com and put “Custom Quote Call” in the subject line

  3. We will work to schedule that call within a couple days

  4. The call typically lasts an hour

  5. The estimate is usually emailed back within a week

STEP 3: GET FINANCING ON OUR FINANCING TAB

Here you will either plug in the amount you get from our Quote Page or the price provided from your Custom Quote.

STEP 4: CONTACT US TO GET INTO THE BUILD QUEUE

Reach out to us at contact@moderntinyliving.com and put “READY TO START” in the subject line!

We will work through final details and send a purchase agreement out to you via DocuSign. Once it has been signed and the 50% deposit has been completed, we will have an in-house meeting about your build followed by an e-intro email connecting you directly to our interior designer or architect if floor plan changes are necessary.

STEP 5: DESIGN TIME

You will spend 1 to 2 weeks working with our interior designer to make sure that your THV tiny house has the exact look and feel you prefer. If you have a custom floor plan, our design team will spend approximately 8 weeks helping you dream up your design. Once you have signed off on the floor plan and design descriptions, we move to the next phase.

STEP 6: BUILD TIME

Usually, by the time you are finished working with our design team, your trailer and windows have been delivered to our shop. We then meet up with our build team and have a meeting with them about your build, showing them your designs and vision. If we aren’t on a long backlog, we will begin framing that week!

STEP 7: MID-BUILD

We require the 2nd payment, taking it from 50% to 75% paid, after completion of the shell. If you chose financing option, this isn’t an issue just keep up with your pay schedule.

STEP 8: COMPLETION

We finish your build and put on the final touches, test all appliances, and slap on that RVIA-certified seal of approval. The final payment is due upon completion but before delivery. The current wait time for a Tiny Home Vision tiny home is approximately 1-3 months.
