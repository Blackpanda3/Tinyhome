THE BASE

THE BASE

Say hello to The Base, the newest variation of our legendary Clover model.

The Base feels similar to one of our earlier Clover builds, Niagara, but features an entirely different color theme and spec within the details. Featuring a full kitchen with oven, full bath, king loft with skylight, and plenty of other little perks, this home was built for full-time tiny house living.

The Base enjoys our full-sized, elevated Modern Tiny Living social area, including its comprehensive storage, bed convertability, and space for socializing. The kitchen features a small oven and cooktop, gorgeous concrete countertops (with adjacent desk/work area), stainless appliances, and our Modern Chrome hardware package. Despite the small footprint of a 24 ft. tiny home, The Base has every amenity you need to make it a travel home, weekend getaway, or full-time residence.
