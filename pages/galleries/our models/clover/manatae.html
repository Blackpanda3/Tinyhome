MANATAE

Ojai is a gorgeous variation of our Kokosing model, a 24 ft. tiny house on wheels. We built Ojai for the legendary Justin Baldoni, an actor and filmmaker whose most recent book, Man Enough, is a bestseller nationwide.

Justin wanted a tiny house for his family out in California, and he chose the nation's leading custom tiny house builder, Modern Tiny Living, for the job. Ojai was built on our famous Kokosing model, but features some absolutely gorgeous wood accents throughout the home that truly set it off.
