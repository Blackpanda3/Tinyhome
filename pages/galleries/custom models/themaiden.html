THE MAIDEN

THE MAIDEN

The Maiden is Modern Tiny Living's latest custom tiny home creation, a 28 ft. x 8.5 ft. tiny house on wheels that is an absolutely breathtaking custom build.

​It features a stunning designer gourmet kitchen, a subway tiled walk-in shower with a glass door, washer/dryer, queen loft, and all the luxuries you could conceivably need in a tiny home on wheels.

​Our customer wanted to have one end of the home open to place whatever furniture she wished, hence the open space at the end! It will feature a beautiful living area, TV, and coffee table. Tiny Home Vision worked hand in hand with this customer to build her dream home, and we are thrilled to have played a part in her tiny house dream!
