Bertha

Say hello to Bertha!

Bertha, named by its owners, is a stunning, big time customization of our Point model with an additional 8 feet in length (or more, if you count the built-on storage at the rear of the home). Bertha features a spectacular office with back door, custom tiled shower, outdoor shower (for post-adventure rinses), and tons of cool storage-nooks and crannies.

Outfitted with a gorgeous concrete countertop, full-size refrigerator/freezer, distressed floors, the elevated Tiny Home Vision social area w/bonus storage, black hardware kit, and much more, Serenity is a truly stunning, spacious, and functional tiny house on wheels with all of the bells and whistles. It's a spectacular example of the customization possibilities that exist when building with Tiny Home Vision.
